About ThermoNoxilo.

Pest control + hygiene improvement in silo bins.

In a silo treatment one or more Thermonoxilo silo heaters are placed in, for example, a flour silo pest control or hygienic improvement (it could be mold, caking, etc.). Only the air in the silo is continuously circulated and heated to a lethal temperature of approximately 55 Celsius. The period of one treatment including the heating phase and the temperature holding phase is about 48 hours.

Silo heater: ThermoNoxilo SO 18.

The thermoNoxilo SO 18 silo heater is entered into the silo via the manhole and by using a specially designed silo winch.

Technical data:

  • Robust aluminum cylinder for placement inside the silo.
  • 18 kW heat output + powerful axial fan.
  • External control cabinet to be placed outside the silo.
  • External control box to place outside the silo.

Silo Winch

With the silo winch two units can be positioned independently inside the silo. All components can be disassembled into manageable sizes and stored on a pallet together with two pieces of ThermoNoxilo.

Applications.

Metal, wood and concrete silos, from 50 m3 to 430 m3.

Safety.

In a flour or even grain silo the occurrence of a dangerous, explosive atmosphere cannot be excluded. Such closed bins are classified according to explosion protection with ATEX zone 21. The ThermoNoxilo and the silo winch are safely designed for your application and tested by DEKRA. In addition to the ATEX certification (type examination) the ThermoNoxilo also has the internationally common IECEx certification.
