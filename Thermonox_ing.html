Functioning.

The ThermoNox® system is based on the principle that food pests such as moths, flour beetles, as well as household pests such as woodworms, bedbugs, mites, clothes moths including their stages of development and eggs are exterminated from safely at a temperature of about 50 °C by protein coagulation.

FOR AN EFFECTIVE DISINFECTION, YOU MUST ALWAYS COMPLY WITH TWO BASIC REQUIREMENTS.

On the one hand, the pest eradication temperature must reach 50 °C – 60 °C and remain constant for long enough, even in corners, holes and also inside machinery. On the other hand, the indicated temperature must not be exceeded in any case, otherwise the implementation of the system would not be suitable due to the damage that excess heat could cause.

THE THERMONOX®SYSTEM IS CLEARLY DIFFERENT FROM OTHER HEAT EXTERMINATION SYSTEMS BY SOLVING THIS PROBLEM IN AN EASY AND EFFECTIVE WAY:

Electric heaters in rooms with danger of dust explosion: Our ThermoNox® disinfection heaters can be introduced directly into the buildings to be disinfected, including also rooms with danger of dust explosion since the maximum temperature is limited inside the ThermoNox heaters. ®. Our system is flexible and adapts to any building or room.

Progressive and slow heating: Through the slow heating of the air, a more profitable energy use is achieved and possible dilation damage caused by heat is thus avoided. Air circulation: The heating of the air in the room is achieved through the circulation of air inside the building. The powerful fans incorporated in the heaters eliminate cold air currents and thus guarantee optimal heat distribution. All gaps are reached as heat retention is achieved over a long distance through air circulation, even behind heavy installations and hard-to-reach cracks.

Thermal regulation: The temperature is controlled directly thanks to 3 thermostats built into each ThermoNox® heater that automatically turn off the heaters when the room temperature is higher than 50 ° C and turn them back on when it is lower. This on and off system is also known as timed. Through this exact temperature control, damage that could be caused by heat is avoided and even highly sensitive electronic equipment and modern electrical installations can be disinfected. Room air: Unlike other thermal systems, which heat cold outside air and blow it into the building (“pipe method”), the ThermoNox® system only heats room air in a closed system.

    • Applications.

    Over the course of the last 15 years of ThermoNox® it has been shown that there are innumerable applications for our system. It is traditionally used in individual compartments, entire buildings or specific areas of a building, however it can also be used in disinfection chambers.

    Sensitive devices such as electronic components, laboratory equipment, cigarette machines (Hauni) or the packaging area are no problem for ThermoNox®. The Swiss machinery and equipment manufacturer Bühler has been certifying that its products can be treated by ThermoNox® for years.

    THE SECTORS IN WHICH THERMONOX® IS MOST COMMONLY USED INCLUDE:

    • Food for animals
      • Pet Food plants, concentrated feed factories.
    • Non-food
      • Tobacco industry, textile, wood pests and disinfection according to the IPPC standard (ISPM 15).

    DRYING BUILDINGS AND ENVIRONMENTAL IMPROVEMENT

    For a few years now, bed bug control has become a sector in which ThermoNox® has proven to be particularly effective:

    • Private homes, residences, official institutions.
    • Hospitality: hotels, motels, youth hostels.
    • Means of transport: train, plane and vehicles.

    LIMITATIONS OF THE USE OF THERMONOX®

    • Disinsection of products (large amounts of products such as grain or flour have an insulating effect).
    • Exterior areas of the factory, permanently open areas.
    • Highly leaky buildings (open roof, loose wood partition).

    Safety.

    THE TREATMENT THERMONOX® OFFERS OUR CUSTOMERS AND USERS THE FOLLOWING GUARANTEES

    • Use in production plants with danger of dust explosion (EU Regulation 94/9/EG, also known as ATEX 100a).
    • Meets the requirements of the ex II 3 D classification.
    • It is controlled and authorized for permanent use in zone 22.

    PREVENTS DAMAGE BY HEAT

    In each ThermoNox® heater there are 3 thermostats that ensure that the temperature for disinsection remains constant between 50º C and 60º C thanks to an automatic on and off system. No heat damage.

    SAFETY AND EFFECTIVENESS.

    The system has been approved by the FSA (Research Institute for the Safety of Applied Systems and Occupational Medicine) for use in the extermination of pests in the cultivation and handling of cereals and flour through heating to about 50°C.
